The Problem

Highway Signs Designed for Garage Sales
Are Marketing Your Industrial Land

Across Ontario's highways and rural corridors, valuable industrial and commercial land is listed with the same hand-painted or low-budget corrugated signs used to sell used furniture. That contradiction sends a signal — and not the one you want.

Serious investors, developers, and commercial tenants do not call phone numbers off roadside signs. They move on. The land sits. The opportunity is lost.

Typical Roadside Sign — Ontario Highway Corridor

XYZ HOLDINGS LTD
LAND FOR LEASE
INDUSTRIAL / DEVELOPMENT
CALL 519-000-0000

  • No context about the opportunity
  • No property details or zoning information
  • Attracts speculative and unqualified calls
  • Signals poor positioning to serious buyers
  • Undervalues the asset being offered
  • No digital follow-through or lead capture
